Who we are?

Welcome to Tidewater Community College's Makerspace & Hackerspace! This is an on-campus club in Virginia Beach. Makerspace allows students to create anything of interest. Also, hackerspace is an area for cybersecurity enthusiasts to improve their skills. Hackerspace is known for having competitions called capture the flag. Anyone with a valid TCC ID is welcome to join the club.

Makerspace & Hackerspace Equipment

The club uses a variety of equipment to fulfill its goals. Some equipment is needed to make a physical item while others are used to produce an intangible product. The club equipment is as follows: 3-D printer, laser etching tool, plotter, and servers.
